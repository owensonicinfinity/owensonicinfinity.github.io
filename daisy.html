DAISY — AI Reasoning Interface

DAISY is an AI-assisted interface designed to translate incomplete, chaotic, or non-technical human input into structured system understanding.

Why DAISY Exists

Most people cannot articulate technical problems precisely. DAISY exists to bridge that gap without requiring the user to “learn the language.”

Core Capabilities

  • Slang and layman input interpretation
  • Intent reconstruction from partial descriptions
  • System-level reasoning support
  • Design, diagnostics, and planning assistance

Future Direction

DAISY is designed as a platform component, not a novelty. Integration with engineering, consulting, and licensing workflows is intentional.
