Daisy OS & Daisy AI

Daisy OS is a full operating system that comes with Daisy AI embedded. Always-on, autonomous, non-intrusive. Daisy AI orchestrates tools for media creation, organization, and management—without hoarding IP or exposing private data.

Users enjoy continuous assistance for daily tasks, automated workflows, and personalized responses. Updates are constant. Security and local context are paramount. Daisy OS exists to ensure no one is ignored, forgotten, or left without clarity.
