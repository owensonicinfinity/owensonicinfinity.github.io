Daisy OS

Daisy OS is our always-on, autonomous operating system that integrates Daisy AI. Daisy AI is embedded within Daisy OS to provide context-aware assistance, task orchestration, and protection of intellectual property. Daisy OS is designed to work seamlessly with biometrics, media creation tools, organization systems, and more, ensuring that user data remains local and secure.
