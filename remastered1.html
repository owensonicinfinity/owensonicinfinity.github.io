Background & Philosophy

Owensonic Infinity: Project Timeline & Risk Disclosure

Our Commitment

We are building on 25+ years of research and development, leveraging decades of DIY innovation and independent experimentation across engineering, materials science, electronics, and product design. Every lesson learned, every prototype tested, and every failure experienced contributes to the product you see today.

Past Successes (and Lessons Learned)

  • High-Performance Clutch for Minibike Engines: Designed and built a superior clutch that outperformed standard options. Unfortunately, the design was copied and commercially exploited by others, teaching us the importance of IP protection and secure development.
  • Industrial Steel Mill Sampler: Developed the largest-ever sampler capable of extracting 8 lbs of molten steel—a breakthrough in cost reduction and efficiency for the steel industry. Despite its success, red tape and corporate barriers prevented full adoption. These experiences shaped our approach to protecting innovation while ensuring deliverable products.

What We’re Offering

Our goal is to consolidate the best of all previous innovations into a single, fully functional, high-quality product package. We aim to deliver the most advanced, reliable, and user-focused solution possible.

Risks & Transparency

Despite our experience and preparation, all new product development carries risks. Some components, features, or experiments may not perform as expected, and unforeseen challenges may arise.

Timeline Flexibility
