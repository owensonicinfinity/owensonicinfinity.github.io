Our Commitment

Past Successes & Lessons Learned

High-Performance Clutch for Minibike Engines: Designed and built a superior clutch that outperformed standard options. Despite success, it was copied and commercially exploited by others. This experience taught us the importance of IP protection and secure development.

Industrial Steel Mill Sampler: Developed the largest-ever sampler capable of extracting 8 lbs of molten steel, a breakthrough for cost reduction and efficiency. Corporate barriers prevented full adoption, teaching us how to protect our innovation while delivering results.
